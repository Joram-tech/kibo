MATHS CHALLENGE

Math Challenge is a virtual prize-based math competition aimed at identifying, engaging, showcasing, and rewarding senior secondary Maths enthusiasts across Africa. The Challenge encourages mathematical reasoning and fluency in using basic mathematical techniques to solve interesting problems. The problems are designed to make students think and the format is designed to encourage collaboration and teamwork. Participating schools nominate teams of 3 students to compete. Schools win cash prizes, and students win Kibo scholarships.
